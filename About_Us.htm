About Us
CommuniLink is a web-based resource that strives to improve the lives of those in need by connecting them with donors in their community.  We are a non-profit resource founded in 2012 by three individuals striving to make a positive impact at the community level after observing a demand for a previously unmet service.

Together, our experience, ambition, and passion will allow us to exceed our goals and grow our organization. We are a web-based resource striving to make a difference in the community through serving as a liaison between parties.  our ultimate mission is to connect those with the ability to provide a "good" to another individual who is in need of a "good."  We will be partnering with organizations to allow us to reach our target market of people with a demonstrated need.
