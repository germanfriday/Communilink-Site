April 3, 2012







 Hello, CommuniLink followers! We are happy to say that the past week has been another incredibly productive one over at CommuniLink. Progress has been make with legal documentation, creation of a business plan, developing branding mechanisms, and brainstorming as we enter our first phase of fundraising.

At this time, we are elated to announce the initial CommuniLink Board of Directors. These three individuals will oversee the organization’s development and progress, each providing their own unique perspective based upon their past experiences. Board members include:

David D’Amico – Attorney at law and small business owner in Schaumburg, IL

Nicole Kreisberg – Masters candidate at the University of Chicago School of Social Service Administration

Sean Redding – Masters candidate at George Washington University

More updates to come later this week, but we wanted to share the news about our board being finalized!
